Before choosing a generation-based route

Group Reality

  • Plan rest stops for families and older travelers
  • Avoid too many district jumps in one day

Customization Plan

  • Use generation picks as a template, not a fixed itinerary
  • Refine meals and hotel choices in Planner
  • Prepare one weather backup route
